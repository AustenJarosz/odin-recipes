Deviled Eggs Recipe

Delicious lookin eggs

Making deviled eggs is a delightful culinary adventure that combines simplicity with creativity. The process begins with the basic act of boiling and peeling eggs, a meditative task that sets the stage for the more exciting steps ahead. Splitting the eggs and scooping out the yolks introduces an element of anticipation, as you prepare the blank canvas for your flavorful masterpiece. The real fun lies in mashing the yolks with mayonnaise, mustard, and a pinch of seasoning, transforming them into a creamy, tangy filling. From here, you can let your imagination run wild with various mix-ins like relish, hot sauce, or even bacon bits. Piping or spooning the filling back into the egg whites adds a satisfying finishing touch, especially when you garnish with paprika, chives, or any colorful herb. Sharing these delectable bites with friends and family, and seeing their faces light up with enjoyment, makes the entire process truly rewarding and fun.

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 3 tbsp of mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp apple cider
  • Salt and pepper
  • Paprika for garnish

Instructions

  1. Boil Eggs for 14 minutes on high
  2. Peel boiled eggs and remove yolks
  3. Mash the yolks in a bowl and add mayo, mustard, vinegar, salt and pepper. Stir
  4. Use a spoon to add mixture back into the hole of the egg
  5. Sprinkle on paprika for garnish
