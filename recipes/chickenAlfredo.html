Chicken Alfredo Recipe

Chicken Alfredoooo

Making Chicken Alfredo is a culinary delight that transforms a few simple ingredients into a rich, creamy dish that feels like an indulgent treat. The process starts with the sizzle of chicken breasts in a hot pan, filling your kitchen with the mouthwatering aroma of seasoned poultry. As the chicken cooks to golden perfection, you begin to create the luxurious Alfredo sauce by melting butter and blending it with heavy cream and freshly grated Parmesan cheese. Stirring the sauce until it becomes velvety smooth is a satisfying experience, especially when the cheese melts into the cream, creating a luscious texture. Combining the tender chicken, perfectly cooked pasta, and creamy sauce brings everything together in a symphony of flavors. Adding a sprinkle of freshly cracked black pepper and a garnish of parsley adds the final flourish. Sharing the deliciously comforting dish with loved ones makes the experience even more enjoyable, turning a simple meal into a memorable culinary adventure.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 12 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • French parsley

Instructions

  1. Season and broth the chicken breasts
  2. Put chicken breasts in oven for 14 minutes at 400f
  3. Boil a pot of salted water and add the fettuccine pasta to the pot and cook until al dente
  4. Drain the pasta and set it aside
  5. Make the alfredo sauce with the butter, garlic, heavy cream, and chicken broth mixed together
  6. Slice the chicken and add all of the products to a bowl
  7. Poor the alfredo sauce over the pasta and chicken
